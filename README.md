## Project Summary

Planning a wedding is one of the most meaningful stages in life, yet it can also be stressful and overwhelming. For Muslim couples in Malaysia, preparation involves more than choosing a venue or attire. Couples must complete religious and legal requirements, prepare documents, attend compulsory courses, manage expenses, and ensure all procedures are finalized before the wedding date. Since marriage procedures differ across states, many couples struggle to find accurate information and keep track of tasks.

Smartphones are now essential tools for managing schedules, finances, and communication. However, most existing wedding planning apps are designed for a general audience and do not address the specific needs of Muslim couples in Malaysia. As a result, couples often rely on multiple sources such as websites, social media, and personal notes, which makes planning more complicated.

The idea for **IjabQabul: Wedding Planner Application** was inspired by these challenges. This app serves as a centralized platform where couples can manage their preparations more effectively. Features include a personalized checklist to organize tasks, a smart timeline that generates schedules based on wedding date and state, and a wedding package comparison tool to evaluate services by price, inclusions, and reviews. The app also provides state-specific marriage guidance to help users understand procedures and legal requirements.

The application is developed using **[Flutter](ca://s?q=Explain_Flutter_framework)** for cross-platform support on Android and iOS, and **[Firebase](ca://s?q=Explain_Firebase_backend)** as the backend for authentication, cloud storage, and real-time synchronization. This combination ensures a reliable, scalable, and user-friendly solution.

In conclusion, IjabQabul simplifies wedding preparation for Muslim couples in Malaysia by offering an organized and accessible digital solution. By integrating planning tools, marriage guidance, and progress tracking in one platform, the app helps reduce stress and supports better decision-making throughout the wedding journey.
